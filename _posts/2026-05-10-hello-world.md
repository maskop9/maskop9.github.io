Welcome.

This is the first post on what I plan to use as a long-running notebook — somewhere to drop write-ups, half-formed research notes, and the occasional rant about Windows internals. If you've landed here from a CTF leaderboard, a conference badge, or a cold email I sent you: hi. The [about page](/) has the elevator pitch.

A loose plan for what shows up here:

- **Red team tradecraft** — payload development, EDR evasion experiments, infrastructure setup notes.
- **Exploit research** — Windows internals, kernel exploration, and reverse engineering walk-throughs.
- **Pentest write-ups** — sanitized findings from public CTFs and vulnerable-by-design environments.
- **The occasional retrospective** — what I've been working on, what I learned, what I broke.

I'd rather post one careful thing than a stream of half-baked ones, so cadence will be irregular. The [RSS feed](/feed.xml) is the best way to keep up.

For everything else — bug bounty disclosures, talk slides, conference shenanigans — there's a [CV](/cv/) and the usual social links at the bottom of the page.

Thanks for reading. More soon.
